# Average Monthly Household Income in Thailand

This dataset presents **average monthly household income in Thailand**, reflecting the economic condition and standard of living of households across different areas of the country.

The data can be used to analyze **income structure, economic inequality, and household income trends** across regions.

Household income refers to **money or goods received by a household** from work, self-production, assets, or assistance from individuals or organizations. Household income can be divided into two main types:

- **Regular income** — income from employment or occupation
- **Irregular income** — income such as prizes, inheritance, or financial assistance

---

# 🏛️ Data Source

- **Source:** National Statistical Office (NSO)
- **Published via:** Open Government Data of Thailand
- **Website:** https://data.go.th/dataset/os_08_00007
- **Data Category:** Household Income and Expenditure Statistics
- **Data Format:** CSV

---

# 📦 Dataset Characteristics

The dataset generally includes the following information:

- Year of the data
- Area (such as **country / region / province**)
- Type of household income
- Average monthly household income
- **Unit:** Baht per month

**Data time range:** approximately **2011 – 2023**
